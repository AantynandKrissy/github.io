Us Now

ABOUT US

We both met at a mutual friend's house at an interesting point in both our lives. It was a small get-together and neither of us really had plans to meet anyone that would be more than a friend or aquaintance.

You'd asked me if I wanted to hang out after and I definitely wanted to. I felt drawn to see you after for a reason I couldn't explain, or remember, and I even suffered through the fact that I had gotten dropped off deep in Marda Loop.

At some point, through both of our frustrations at my driver's error, I had decided it would be a great idea to send you a zodiac compatability write up. Lucky for me you didn't remember that fact until later.

Fortunately your best friend Graham was there to make sure you were okay, and his presence gave us a reason to take the time to get to know eachother more. I feel like that was a major reason we ended up together.

We had an interesting start to everything. On a complete whim, I believed you'd like to take a trip to a museum since there was an exhibit I thought was cool there. It turned out to be your first time going to one, and you said you had a great time.

I have fun with you just going to Boli; I have fun with you when we see your mentors; I have a blast with you watching Netflix; and I look forward to simple things like meeting your mom. I feel like I'm perfect the way I am when you're next to me.

I hope this website serves as a constant source of happiness as we add memories and magic to the time we have together. I know that, together, we can commit our memory to the stars and live there for eternity.

Some Favourite Moments

Moments With You Are Amazing

The Moon

Seeing you light up when you saw the moon painting at Glenbow was the first time I noticed how much your smile affected me in a good way. Sometimes I feel like it's going to be a hard act to follow from that day forward. I always want to make you smile like that, and nothing makes me happier than knowing it's a new experience for you that you actually enjoy.

Thanksgiving

It's unlikely that I'll ever forget the day we decided to make a thanksgiving dinner since we weren't going to be with family. We nailed it baby, and here's to many more times we hit the nail right on the head. We might not always be in sync but I think that's why we work.

Lucy In The Sky

The first time we decided to take our minds to the next level was a rollercoaster I will never forget. Maybe it was the culmination of everything that night, maybe it was just something we needed to do. There were times I was worried we'd gone far off the rails but the night ended with us both saying I Love You.

Haunting of Hill House

You had asked me if I wanted to watch this show with you, and the concept seemed pretty new. Who'd have thought that the show would turn out to be one of my favourites, and resonate on so many levels. Somehow it feels like that damn show made me understand life better, and brought me closer to you. That may have had a bit to do with the k day.

Every Dream

You have quieted the storms of my mind enough to make me sleep on more nights than I can ever remember in my modern life. Having you beside me is like being gently submerged in a sensory deprivation tank that helps your mind melt away into a dimention of dreams and peace. I can even have my face in your back and not be bothered by the warm air, that's 100% new.

Lucy 2 With You

I have never had the vibes so good as they were that last evening. That one comedian was on fire, and almost every movie after that was perfect and kept us light as helium. Nobody has ever been so complimentary to my own energy that I don't feel my mood change with overpowering empathy. You are largely a mystery to me, but I'd never be happy knowing all about you. I am happy that I get a chance to try though.
